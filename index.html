Controls


Use arrows to move
Space bar to shoot
Z: Drop MegaBomb
P: Pause game

Powerups

There are five powerups
Green arrow is more damage
Yellow double arrows is increased rate of fire
Two blue bullets is double shoot
Three blue bullets is triple shoot
Megabomb is extra MegaBomb
Red cross is extra health

Map editor

To use the map editor,
select the enemy you want to add and
place it on the grid.
Use the arrow keys to move the
grip up and down. Once you are
done, press Save, Exit,
and launch a new game!
